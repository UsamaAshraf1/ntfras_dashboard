Executive Brief — National Strategic Value Assessment

Demand exists. Capital is willing.
The bridge to deployment is broken.

Pakistan's robotics and autonomous systems sector is held back not by appetite — industry wants to deploy, capital is poised to flow, foreign buyers are ready to source — but by an absent integrator class that should connect NCRA's research to industry's shop floor. The National Strategic Value Score sits at 2.6 / 5 — a Capability Contributor classification. Three irreversible Day-90 actions move the score above 3.0 and unlock USD 1.65 billion of additional capital deployment between Year 1 and Year 3.
